    Learn About Loans

    What is a Loan?

    A loan is money borrowed from a bank or agency, repaid with interest over time.

    Private vs. Government Banks

    Private banks (e.g., HDFC) often have competitive rates but stricter eligibility. Government banks (e.g., SBI) offer schemes for all, with subsidies for certain groups.

    Tips for Choosing

    • Check interest rates and hidden fees.
    • Ensure you meet eligibility (income, credit score).
    • Compare pre-approval offers.
