About LoanSaathi

Welcome to LoanSaathi, your trusted companion for navigating the world of loans! Launched as an MCA mini project, this website simplifies loan comparisons from private banks (like HDFC and ICICI) and government agencies (like SBI and NABARD). Whether you're seeking personal, home, business, or education loans, we provide tools and insights to help you make informed decisions.

Our Mission

To empower users with transparent, accessible financial tools, promoting literacy and confidence in loan selection. We bridge the gap between complex banking options and everyday needs, ensuring no one feels overwhelmed by financial choices.

Key Features

  • Loan Comparison Tool: Input your details to get personalized recommendations with interest rates and eligibility.
  • Government Schemes: Explore subsidized programs like MSME loans, student credit cards, and education loans.
  • Credit Score Check: Simulate your score and get tips for improvement.
  • Educational Content: Learn about loans, private vs. government options, and best practices.
  • API Integration: Real-time data from government portals for accuracy.
